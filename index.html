Texas Pre-K Programs

Why Pre-K?

Studies show that participation in Pre-K programs can have a significant impact on a child's future.

Children who attend Pre-K are:

  • More likely to graduate high school
  • More likely to enroll in college
  • More likely to be employed
  • More likely to earn higher wages

For more information on the benefits of Pre-K programs, check out the Resources section at the bottom of the page.

How does this tool help?

This tool provides a single location to get information about Pre-K programs in Texas. The data shown below focuses on a few key variables of each program:

  • Median class size
  • Student - teacher ratio
  • Whether full-day or half-day scheduling is offered
  • Curriculum offered (for more information on this, see the Curriculums section below.)
  • Percentage of enrolled students eligible for ADA accomodations
